Rajma Recipe

Rajma is one of the most loved main course dishes from Punjabi cuisine. This North Indian recipe serves both as a party recipe and regular dinner dish, and is best enjoyed with rice. Rajma or kidney beans are rich in protein and ideal for those who prefer healthy and balanced diet.

Ingredients of Rajma

4 Servings
2 cup red kidney beans
3 chopped tomato
1 teaspoon powdered garam masala powder
2 tablespoon sunflower oil
1 handful stem removed coriander leaves
2 tablespoon coriander powder
1 tablespoon spice black pepper
1 chopped onion
1/2 teaspoon finely chopped ginger
1 pinch powdered turmeric
2 cup Water
1 teaspoon powdered salt
1 tablespoon cumin powder
How to make Rajma
Step 1
Wash the kidney beans and boil them for half an hour in pressure cooker till they are tender. Do not throw away the water.

Step 2
Take a pan and heat oil in it over moderate flame. Add onions and cook till they are golden brown. Now add ginger, garlic and tomatoes and cook on medium flame, stirring continuously till they become soft, squishy and light brown.

Step 3
Now add 2 tbsp coriander powder, 1 tbsp cumin powder and 1 tbsp black pepper powder to the mixture.

Step 4
Add salt and turmeric and mix well. Cook till oil starts separating from the mixture. Now add garam masala and mix.

Step 5
Add cooked kidney beans to this mixture. Now add the water that you had kept aside. Cook covered on low flame for ten minutes. Turn off the heat and transfer rajma in a serving bowl.

Step 6
Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
